About Us – GrowKit PH

GrowKit PH is a fun, hands-on planting experience that inspires kids and young adults to grow their own food and understand where it comes from—from seed to harvest. Our easy, clean, and exciting kits are perfect for homes, schools, and family bonding.

We believe kids learn best by doing. Growing plants builds patience, responsibility, curiosity, and healthier habits—proving that small gardens can grow big dreams.

Parents love GrowKit PH because it helps children stay productive, practice responsibility, develop science and nature awareness, build confidence, and form good habits early.

More than a planting kit, GrowKit PH is a life-skill starter kit. We want every young Filipino to proudly say, “I grew this!”

Together, let’s grow food, knowledge, and a better future—one plant at a time.
